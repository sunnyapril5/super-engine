ViViaN

Virtual Visiting Nurse!

A virtual voice interactive personal care assistant at home - think Amazon Alexa

ViViaN creates a trusted relationship over time with the elderly patient who is a newly diagnosed diabetic.


She speaks and relates to the patient's needs and concerns.
She creates a closed loop system between the patient, clinical care team, and family.
She focuses on the four pillars of diabetic care management - medication adherence, insulin compliance, proper diet, and exercise through positive reinforcement, health education, and reminders.

Initially, we are focusing on newly diagnosed elderly diabetic patients transitioning from acute care to home setting. This population may have issues in maintaining clinical treatment regiments. This population tends to have memory and cognitive functioning issues.

An example of ViViaN using Amazon Alexa:

She will reference medical records and psychosocial information to create patient profile.

She will interact with the patient to provide reminders, alerts and encouragement like:

Take medications

Administer glucose testing

Exercise and diet reminders

She can provide medical training updates for reinforcement.

She can send alerts to nurse, physician, and family caregiver.

She can reorder medications and testing supplies when running low.
