Palau

Pristine Paradise!

It is scenically Magical!

It's hard not to be overwhelmed by its extraordinary array of natural wonders:


Palau is an archipelago of about 200 largely pristine limestone and volcanic islands, blanketed in emerald forest, surrounded by a shimmering turquoise lagoon...

Palau is famous for its underwater world.

World Class Snorkeling and Diving in the Rock Islands

And famous for Jellyfish Lake...Snorkeling in Jellyfish Lake is a once in a lifetime experience.

Once you dipped into Jellyfish Lake, it is Surreal:

You can Touch the stingless jellyfish.

You can Swim with them.

You can Look at them as they swim by you.

In this lake, the jellyfish have adapted to the new conditions by losing their sting.

The Stingless Jellyfish gets suntan or sunbathe in the afternoon.

At night the jellyfish swim to the bottom of the lake and get fed
